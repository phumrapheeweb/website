About Phumrapee Engineering

Phumrapee Engineering Systems Co., Ltd. was established in 2013 as a leading provider of electrical and electronic equipment for residential, commercial, and marine applications. With over a decade of experience, we have expanded our services to include complete electrical system installations, communication systems, sanitary systems, and air conditioning solutions.

Our expert team of engineers and technicians delivers professional installation services for hotels, condominiums, and commercial buildings throughout Phuket and Krabi, ensuring the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability.
